#### Mouse Valley

A Mod for Stardew Valley. 一个为《星露谷物语》编写的模组。

The first time you enter the mine, you will find something weird.......Go and find out what it is! Then you can become friends with four cute mice and help them build their valley. 当你第一次进入矿井，你可能会发现一些奇怪的事……去查清它们！然后你将可以和四只可爱的老鼠成为朋友，帮助它们建设它们的山谷。

As you spend time with the mice, you will discover there was once an ancient mouse civilization. You'll start to wonder: why are only four of them here? what happened to their civilization? 在你与老鼠们相处的时光里，你将了解到曾经存在一个古老的老鼠文明，为什么现在只剩下四只老鼠了？老鼠文明发生了什么？

The mice will have babies! You can watch them grow up. They will have their own hobbies and will face many problems and troubles along the way. Maybe you can help them make their dreams come true? 老鼠们将会有孩子！你可以关注它们成长，它们将会有自己的兴趣爱好，在成长中遇到许多问题与烦恼。或许你可以帮助它们实现它们的梦想？

Fourteen NPC mice in our planning! Each of them will have a complete storyline to help you immerse yourself in the life of Stardew Valley alongside the mice. 计划中的十四只 NPC 老鼠！它们中的每一只都将有一条完整的故事线，让你沉浸在与老鼠们一起的星露谷生活中。

New maps! A secret valley behind the mountains. You may find its entrance somewhere in the mine. 新地图！一 个群山背后的秘密山谷，你或许能在矿井中的某处找到它的入口。

New crops! The mice will bring their own unique crops to the valley. But they aren't just your average vegetable...... 新作物！老鼠们将把它们独有的作物带到山谷中。但它们可不只是一些蔬菜……

New shops! Seeds for these mouse crops can be found here. And what's more......新商店！你可以在这里找到老鼠作物的种子。还有什么呢……

New weapons! Are you curious about how mice fight? 新武器！你是否对老鼠如何战斗感到好奇？

New fish in planning! Come and show off your fishing skills! 计划中的新的鱼！来展示你钓鱼的技艺吧！

New festivals! They will be a great chance to learn more about mice...... 新节日！这将会是了解老鼠们的好机会。

The mod is still a work in progress, but I will keep working on it and adding more interesting things. Thanks for your support!  The finished version of Mouse Valley will be here soon! 这个模组目前只是一个半成品，但我将保持工作并加入更多有意思的东西。感谢你的支持，敬请期待完整版的《老鼠山谷》！
